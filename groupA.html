Scenario 1 - Commitment & Consistency
An attacker using the Commitment & Consistency principle has created a fake charity extremely similar to current ones and is sending out emails asking for people to donate via a link. The link leads the unsuspecting victim to a site that is identical. Once they have entered their login credentials and card information, the attacker now has that sensitive data.
Explanation:
The attacker has used the principle of commitment and consistency by creating an identical webpage to an already existing charity via a phishing link, catching people who want to donate to the charity and gaining access to their personal information and bank details.
