Scenario 3 - Liking
An attacker using the Liking principle will possibly impersonate a fellow colleague or employee the victim knows well by creating a near identical email or WhatsApp contact to get them to send over documents they "forgot" or have gone "missing". Because the victim might know this individual on a personal basis they would be willing to send over the documents to help them not get in trouble or help them finish work that wasn't completed in the office.
Explanation
The attacker has used the principle of liking to receive sensitive data that the victim wouldn't have sent if they received the message from a random person, but with the principle of liking, people are more inclined to help their fellow employees/friends.
