Scenario 2 - Authority
An attacker using the Authority principle uses a near perfectly identical email of the target's boss requesting that they send specific files to them immediately. Once the employee sees the email, they will not hesitate in sending the requested files, transferring sensitive data to the attacker.
Explanation:
The attacker has used the principle of Authority to gain unauthorized access to sensitive company data as the employee was thinking that it was their boss requesting the files whereas it was actually the attacker.
